Resources

Tools and information to support your journey.

Managing Stress

  • Practice deep breathing for 5 minutes daily
  • Break large tasks into smaller, manageable steps
  • Set realistic expectations for yourself
  • Take regular breaks during study sessions
  • Talk to someone you trust about your pressures

Family Conflicts

  • Choose calm moments for difficult conversations
  • Use "I" statements to express feelings
  • Listen to understand, not to respond
  • Accept that you can't control others' reactions
  • Set healthy boundaries with kindness

Study Pressure

  • Your worth is not defined by grades alone
  • Create a realistic study schedule with breaks
  • Focus on progress, not perfection
  • Seek help from teachers or peers when needed
  • Remember: exams are a part, not the whole, of life

Building Resilience

  • Acknowledge your feelings without judgment
  • Practice self-compassion daily
  • Build a support network you can rely on
  • Learn from setbacks, they are teachers
  • Celebrate small wins along the way
